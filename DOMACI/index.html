Cookbook

A cookbook or cookery book is a kitchen reference containing recipes. Cookbooks may be general, or may specialize in a particular cuisine or category of food. Recipes in cookbooks are organized in various ways: by course (appetizer, first course, main course, dessert), by main ingredient, by cooking technique, alphabetically, by region or country, and so on. They may include illustrations of finished dishes and preparation steps; discussions of cooking techniques, advice on kitchen equipment, ingredients, and substitutions; historical and cultural notes; and so on.

Easy Sugar Cookies

Cookies

Quick and easy sugar cookies! They are really good, plain or with candies in them. My friend uses chocolate mints on top, and they're great!

Ingredients:

  1. 2¾ cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. ½ teaspoon baking powder
  4. 1 cup butter, softened
  5. 1½ cups white sugar
  6. 1 egg
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Guacamole

Guacamole recipe

You can make this avocado salad smooth or chunky depending on your tastes.

Ingredients:

  1. avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  2. 1 lime, juiced
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  5. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  6. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  7. 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)
